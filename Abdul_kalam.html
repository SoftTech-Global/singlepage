APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Here's a time line of President kalam's life:

  • 1931He was born at Rameshwaram, Tamilnadu.
  • 1954Graduated from Saint Joseph's Collage, Tiruchippalli.
  • 1955Enrolled at MIT to study Aerospace Engineering.
  • 1960Joined DRDO as Chief Scientist.
  • 1965Started working on rocket projects independently at DRDO.
  • 1969He was transferred to ISRO.
  • 1980-90Responsible for the development of AGNI and PRITHVIMissile
  • 1981He was awarded with Padam BhusanGovernment of India.
  • 1990He was awarded with Padam VibhusanGovernment of India.
  • 1992-99Served as the Chief Scientific Advisorto the PM and the Secretary of DRDO.
  • 1997He was awarded with Bharat RatnaGovernment of India.
  • 2002-07Served as 11th President of India.
  • 2012Launched mission for the young of the nation called "What Can I Give Movement"
  • 2015Died at the age of 83 Years.
